Welcome to ILAT

ILAT is an educational institution that focuses on teaching languages and professional training. The institute aims to be the top institution in Somaliland providing language and in-service training.

ILAT is dedicated to providing education that meets international standards. To ensure that its training meets the required benchmarks, the college follows the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFRL), which offers a comprehensive guide for language teaching, learning, and assessment.
